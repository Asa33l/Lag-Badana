Why Lag Badana

Forests, wildlife and coast worth defending

Lag Badana is home to important forests, wildlife and coastal ecosystems that are vital for biodiversity and local livelihoods.

Our work helps ensure these natural resources are protected for future generations — keeping the land productive, the wildlife safe and the coastline resilient.

Where life flourishes

From flowering wetlands to ancient coastal forest, every habitat here supports a web of life — and the people who depend on it. Protecting Lag Badana means protecting all of it.
